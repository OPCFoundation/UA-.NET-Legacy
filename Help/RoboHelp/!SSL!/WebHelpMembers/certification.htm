Certification Overview

The OPC Foundation Certification program exists to help vendors maximize their product's potential by:

  • verifying compliance to the specifications

  • validating interoperability with 3rd party systems/applications

  • validating the usability of the product: installation, removal, configuration, documentation etc.

  • validating robustness, recovery, and resource efficiency.

All Unified Architecture products are required to become lab certified. The certification of a product is conducted within a Test Lab that is sanctioned by the OPC Foundation. Testing within a lab is based on a first-come-first-served basis, so book now and book early to reserve your desired time-slot. The testing of a product within the North American test lab is currently free to members (subject to change).

For more information on certification visit the OPC Foundation website:
